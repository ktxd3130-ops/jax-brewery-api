## Description (max 4000 chars)
```
Pint helps you find the right brewery — right now.

Most beer apps hand you a list and let you sort it out. Pint decides with you. Tell it your mood, what you're hungry for, and how far you'll go, and it ranks the breweries that actually fit the moment — open now, good for your group, pouring the styles you want.

WHAT MAKES PINT DIFFERENT

• Right-Now picks — One tap surfaces the best breweries open this minute, ranked with the reasons why ("dog-friendly patio," "live music tonight," "kitchen open till 10").

• Food-first scoring — Hungry? Pint weighs the kitchen, not just the taps. Full menu, food trucks, or snacks-only — it knows the difference and ranks accordingly.

• Weather-aware — Rooftop on a clear evening, cozy taproom when it's pouring. Pint factors the forecast into what it recommends.

• Near you, for real — Uses your location to sort by distance and drive time. Deny location and it falls back to a curated local list — never a blank screen.

• Map view — See every brewery around you on an interactive map. Tap for details and one-tap directions.

• The full picture — Hours, what each place is known for, food, dog- and kid-friendly flags, current taps, events, photos, and reviews in a single tap-to-open card.

• Works on the go — Your local brewery list is built in, so the core experience loads instantly and keeps working with a weak signal.

BUILT FOR JACKSONVILLE, GROWING BEYOND
Pint launches with a hand-curated, regularly verified Jacksonville guide and expands to more cities — so it stays accurate instead of rotting into a list of closed taprooms.

Pour smarter. Download Pint and fill your pint.

—
Pint is an informational guide for craft-beer fans 21+. Please drink responsibly.
```
